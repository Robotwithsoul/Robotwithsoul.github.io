Research Experience

 

•  Robot-assisted System for CT-guided Liver Radiofrequency Ablation

•  Color-coded Digital Subtraction Angiography (DSA)

Developed software in Matlab to implement color coding of digital subtraction angiography based on local DSA data. Results from this can show time information of DSA in a single color image.

Implemented a Demons registration method to reduce motion artifacts and make color-coded images clearer.
