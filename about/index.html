Research Experience

 

        The robot-assisted system for CT-guided liver Radiofrequency ablation

Robot and treatment planning system is included in this project to make the liver Radiofrequency ablation more accurate. Design of treatment planning system is my main task.

So far, a software prototype for treatment planning has been developed and following functions are included: medical image processing (basic segmentation and registration methods), medical image visualization and manual trajectory planning.

A registration method of pre-operative enhanced CT image to intra-operative CT images during CT-guided liver ablation is being developed.

Tsinghua University

        Color-coded Digital subtraction angiography (DSA)

Developed a software prototype in Matlab to implement color coding of digital subtraction angiography based on the local DSA data. Results from this can show time information of DSA in one color image.

Implemented a Demons registration method to reduce motion artifact and make color-coded image clearer.

 

Neusoft Medical Systems, Beijing, China
